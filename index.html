Story

For over 60 years, Marshall’s legacy has grown from code to code, person to person, generation to generation. It all began in 1962, when Jim Marshall built his first amplifier. Since then, we’ve never stopped amplifying music for artists and fans. With each passing decade, we’ve refined our craft to ensure our reputation for legendary sound and iconic design remains unrivaled.

Guitars
